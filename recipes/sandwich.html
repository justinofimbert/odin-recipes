Sandwich

a sandwich of milanesa is a typical tucuman food, where we put a milanesa(a steak covered by pan rallado) between a piece of bread sliced in two, over this milanesa we put different vegetables and dressings

Ingredients

  • bread
  • one or two milanesas
  • lettuce
  • a tomato
  • onions
  • mayo
  • mustard
  • hot chili sauce

optional

  • one or two eggs
  • ham
  • cheese

Steps

  1. cook the milanesa
  2. if we want "el especial" add (cooked) eggs ham and cheese
  3. slice bread in two and place the milanesa between the two slices
  4. place lettuce first, then tomato slices, fried onions and dressings
  5. cut in half and setup in plate
